A Digital Experiment

We wanted to push the limits of HTML5 and amazing technologies such as Electron, Cordova, Ionic, and Docker in order to create one codebase to run on all platforms. This has been amazingly fun and we hope to benefit the community from our efforts.

Free Music

There's a ton of public domain music on the Internet. There are a ton of players for them as well. We just wouldn't be a decent music player if we didn't make it so you could search and collect it all in one place.

Built In Cloud Storage

Easily configure the app the save all your music to your favorite cloud storage provider or to local disk. You can configure this globally or per user.

  • Amazon S3

Metadata Plugins

The app is able to pull from multiple source of data to find the information you need about your music.

  • Iguana

Nerding Out
